Mesquite Chicken

Description

This is an amazing chicken recipe that doesn't take too long to make and can be served it a variety of ways. My favorite is to simply serve it over rice with a vegetable or two on the side.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 lbs boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 packets mesquite seasoning
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup jasmine rice

Directions

  1. Cut chicken breasts into small strips or chunks
  2. Place chicken into large ziplock bag
  3. In a small bowl, add mesquite seasoning, water, and oil
  4. Whisk marinade thouroughly
  5. Add marinade mix to ziplock bag with chicken
  6. Let chicken marinate in fridge for at least 15 minutes. For best results, leave overnight.
  7. Cook chicken in skillet on medium high heat until thouroughly cooked.
  8. Serve over rice with a side of vegetables.
  9. Enjoy!
