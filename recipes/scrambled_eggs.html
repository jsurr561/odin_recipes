Scrambled Eggs

Description

Who doesn't like some good scrambled eggs? This is a simple recipe to make some great scrambled eggs, best served with a side of toast, bacon, and orange juice.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 Large Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Whole Milk
  • Finely shredded cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together eggs, salt, pepper, and a splash of milK until egg whites are well mixed in
  2. Preheat skillet to high heat
  3. Add egg mixture to skillet
  4. Add desired amount of cheddar cheese
  5. Stir consistently to achieve a fluffy texture
  6. Do not overcook, eggs should be light and fluffy, not smushed
  7. Add eggs to slice of toast and enjoy!
