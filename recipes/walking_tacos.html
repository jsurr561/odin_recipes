Walking Tacos

Description

This is an easy recipe perfect for when you want something tasty without much effort. Just like tacos, it is very cusotomizeable to individual taste. I always keep ingredients on hand to make this recipe in case I get a hankering for it, or just want a hot meal without expending much energy cooking.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • Individual size bags of Doritos or Fritos chips
  • Sour Cream
  • Shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • Other taco toppings as desired

Directions

  1. Cook ground beef in skillet on medium high heat until no pink remains
  2. Reduce heat and add 1/2 cup water and taco seasoning
  3. Let simmer 2 minutes
  4. Open bag of preferred chips then squeeze the bag to crush them
  5. Add ground beef, cheese, and sour cream to chip bag and stir
  6. Add other taco toppings as desired
  7. Enjoy!
